What is Afro-House Music?

Afro-House (also written as Afro House) is a sub-genre of house music mainly developed in South Africa. The genre emerged in the 1990s. This musical style fuses elements of traditional house with African rhythms and sounds, incorporating instruments such as the saxophone, piano and synthesizers, as well as vocals in various African languages. (Wikipedia)

Prominent artists known for their Afro house music include Black Coffee, Keinemusik, Nitefreak, and Caiiro, among many others (Splice).
