Course Reflection

Throughout HCI 406, I've gained valuable skills and knowledge that have transformed my approach to web development. Beginning with the fundamentals of HTML structure and text formatting, I progressed through increasingly complex concepts including responsive design implementation and framework utilization.

The assignments provided practical experience with each concept, building upon previous knowledge to create more sophisticated web elements. The final project allowed me to synthesize these skills into a cohesive, professional website that demonstrates my ability to create user-friendly, accessible, and visually appealing web experiences.

What I found most valuable was learning how to approach design from a user-centered perspective, ensuring the aesthetic choices enhance rather than hinder usability. I look forward to applying these principles in future projects and continuing to develop my skills as a web designer and developer.
